Bank management system

MoneyMate

This system is designed to manage all essential banking operations such as account creation, deposit and withdrawal transactions, balance inquiries, and account management. It provides a secure and user-friendly interface for both bank staff and customers, helping to streamline daily banking tasks and ensure efficient record-keeping.

Technologies used:

C#, GUNA2.UI Framework, SQL Server, SQL Server management studio

Highlights

  • Account Creation and Management
  • Deposit and Withdrawal Handling
  • Fund Transfer Between Accounts
  • Loan Application and Management
  • Customer Information Management
  • Transaction History and Statements
  • Secure Login and Role-Based Access
